Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

The first step is an initial consultation. You can schedule one by filling out our contact form or calling us directly. During this meeting, we'll discuss your vision, requirements, budget, and timeline to see how we can best help you.

Project timelines can vary greatly depending on the scope and complexity. A single room design might take a few weeks, while a full home or commercial renovation can take several months. We provide a detailed project timeline after the initial design concept is approved.

We offer a flexible fee structure to suit different project needs. This can be a flat fee, an hourly rate, or a percentage of the project cost. We are transparent about all costs, and the fee structure will be clearly outlined in our project proposal.

Yes, we offer full project management services. We coordinate with all necessary contractors, suppliers, and artisans to ensure the design is executed flawlessly, on time, and within budget, allowing you to have a stress-free experience.
